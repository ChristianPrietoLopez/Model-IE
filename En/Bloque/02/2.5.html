2.5 – Summary of Energetic, Quantum and Cosmological Formulas

This section compiles key expressions of the IE Model:

  • Energy density: ρ_IE(t) = ρ_∞ + (ρ₀ − ρ_∞) / [1 + (t / t_s)^α]
  • Hubble parameter: H(t) = √ρ_IE(t)
  • Scale factor: a(t) = a₀ · exp(∫ H(t) dt)
  • Redshift: z(t) = 1 / a(t) − 1
  • Luminosity distance: d_L(z) = (1 + z) · ∫ dz'/H(z')
  • Primordial spectrum: P(k) = k^−β · exp(−λ_IE · k)
  • Spectral index: n_s = −β − λ_IE · k
  • Quantum state: |Ψ_rem(t)> = Σ c_n(t) |Ψ_n>
  • Entanglement entropy: S(t) = −Tr[ρ_red(t) log ρ_red(t)]
