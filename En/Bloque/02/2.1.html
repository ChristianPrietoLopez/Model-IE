2.1 – Base Energy Function: ρ_IE(t)

The energy density of the IE Model is defined as a smooth, decreasing function:

ρ_IE(t) = ρ_∞ + (ρ₀ − ρ_∞) / [1 + (t / t_s)^α]

This expression describes a continuous energy evolution without inflation, where:

  • ρ₀: initial density
  • ρ_∞: residual stabilizing density
  • t_s: characteristic time scale
  • α: decay exponent
