2.8 – Validation from the Lagrangian

Starting from the Lagrangian:

L = a³(t) [ (1/2)(ȧ/a)² − ρ_IE(t) ]

The variational principle yields:

a·ä + (1/2)ȧ² + 3a²·ρ_IE(t) = 0

This formally validates the emergent dynamic of a(t), confirming that:

  • a·ä represents acceleration
  • (1/2)ȧ² is the kinetic term
  • 3a²·ρ_IE(t) acts as an effective energetic force

Consistent with ȧ/a = √ρ_IE(t), reinforcing the IE framework.
