2.7 – Radiation Inclusion in the IE Model

Total energy density is modified as:

ρ_tot(t) = ρ_IE(t) + ρ_r,0 / a(t)^4

This allows modeling the early universe:

  • a(t) ∝ [ρ₀ / ρ_IE(t)]^(1/3), normalized to a(t₀) = 1
  • (1 + z) = 1 / a(t)
  • d_L(z) = (1 + z) × ∫[t(z) → t₀] (c / a(t')) dt'
  • μ(z) ≈ 5 log₁₀(d_L / 10 pc)

Model parameters are fitted by minimizing χ². For α ≈ 2, the IE Model mimics a matter-dominated universe.
